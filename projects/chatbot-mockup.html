A Chatbot UI Mockup for the City of Mendrisio

A clean, responsive graphical interface prototype for a municipal chatbot, designed to facilitate citizen interaction with local government services.

Demo

Project details

The project focused on creating a high-fidelity, interactive mockup of a chatbot interface tailored for the City of Mendrisio. The goal was to provide an intuitive, accessible, and visually aligned user experience that reflects the city's branding while enabling citizens to quickly access information and services (such as permits, events, and public office hours). The mockup was developed as a standalone frontend prototype to validate design decisions and user flows before full integration with backend logic.

Technologies used

  • HTML, CSS, Typescript
  • Vite (bundler)
  • TailwindCSS (framework)
